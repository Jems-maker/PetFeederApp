# 🐾 PawCare

**PawCare** is an all-in-one, modern mobile application designed to simplify and elevate the experience of managing your pet's life. Combining a beautifully designed, premium Tailwind-inspired interface with smart automation, PawCare ensures your furry friends are fed on time, every time, while keeping their health records meticulously organized.

---

## 🌟 Key Benefits & Features

### 1. Smart Feeding & Analytics
Say goodbye to guessing if your pet has been fed. PawCare provides a real-time smart dashboard that tracks your pet's dietary habits.
* **Dynamic Analytics Hub**: View your feeding *Consistency Score*, total success rates, and a beautiful weekly bar chart tracking feeding frequency.
* **Remote "Feed Now"**: Instantly deploy food to your smart feeder with a single tap, no matter where you are.
* **Visual Meal Logs**: Keep an organized, easy-to-read history of every successful and failed feeding attempt.

### 2. Complete Health & Wellness Tracking
PawCare goes beyond the food bowl to provide a holistic view of your pet's well-being.
* **Dedicated Health Dashboard**: A unique, separated screen solely focused on health management.
* **Vaccination & Deworming Reminders**: Safely log dates for essential shots and routine care so you never miss a milestone.
* **Vet Visit Logs**: Record past appointments and schedule upcoming checkups directly in the app.

### 3. Automated Scheduling
Your pet's routine is safe with us.
* **Custom Meal Slots**: Set specific times of the day (Morning, Noon, Afternoon, Evening) for automated food dispensing.
* **Offline Hardware Syncing**: Built-in support to sync schedules with ESP/hardware controllers over Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, ensuring meals are dispensed even if your phone isn't connected.

### 4. Premium, Modern User Experience
* **Tailwind-Inspired Design**: Enjoy gorgeous, rounded white cards with soft floating shadows, making the app a joy to look at and use.
* **Personalized Profiles**: The dashboard greets you by name and prominently displays your pet's avatar.
* **Smart Notifications & Updates**: Helpful red dot indicators on your "Profile" tab let you know instantly if there is a pending app or firmware update.
